About the character

Jeremy Allen White (born February 17, 1991, Brooklyn, New York, USA) is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Philip "Lip" Gallagher in the television series Shameless (2011 - 2021). Life and career In elementary school, White was involved in dance, specifically ballet, jazz and tap. Jeremy made his debut as an actor in 2006, starring in the television series Persuasion and the movie Beautiful Ohio Personal life Allen is in a relationship with actress Addison Timlin. On October 18, 2019, the couple married. Their daughter, Ezer Billie White, was born on October 20, 2018. Ezer's godmother was actress Dakota Johnson.
